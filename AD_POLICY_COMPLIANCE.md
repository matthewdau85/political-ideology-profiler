# Ad Policy Compliance

## Placement Rules
- No ads that block critical quiz interactions.
- No deceptive ad styling that mimics product controls.
- No interstitial interruption during active answer flow.

## Sensitive Content Controls
- Review political content risk for advertiser safety.
- Remove/flag ad placements near sensitive user-generated contexts if applicable.

## Prohibited Categories
Configure ad provider blocklists for categories inconsistent with product trust and policy goals.

## Complaint Handling
- Provide contact channel for ad complaints.
- Acknowledge within 2 business days.
- Escalate policy violations to ad ops owner.
