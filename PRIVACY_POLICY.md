# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-07

## 1. Controller and Contact
Political Ideology Profiler (the "Service") controls personal data described in this policy.

Contact for privacy requests:
- Email: privacy@your-domain.com
- Web form: /contact (select "Privacy Request")

## 2. Scope
This policy covers data processed through:
- Website and PWA
- Account/authentication flows
- Payment and entitlement services
- Support and contact flows

## 3. Data Categories
### 3.1 Account data
- Email address
- Supabase user identifier
- Account timestamps

### 3.2 Quiz and profile data
- Economic/social scores
- Typology and derived dimensions
- Top issues, country, age band (if provided)
- Methodology and quiz version metadata

### 3.3 Payment/entitlement data
- Stripe event/session/subscription identifiers
- Purchased feature and entitlement status
- Payment amount/currency metadata

### 3.4 Technical and security data
- API request metadata required for security operations
- Error/diagnostic logs

## 4. Purposes and Legal Bases
We process data for:
- Service delivery (account login, result persistence, premium access)
- Security and abuse prevention
- Billing and entitlement lifecycle management
- Support and incident response
- Aggregated analytics and product improvement (where consent/legitimate basis applies)

## 5. Data Sharing and Processors
We use processors/subprocessors for hosting, auth, billing, analytics, and communications.
Core providers include Vercel, Supabase, Stripe, and configured analytics/email providers.

We do not sell personal data.

## 6. International Transfers
Data may be processed in regions where our providers operate.
Where required, we apply contractual safeguards provided by vendors.

## 7. Retention
- Account/profile data: retained while account remains active.
- Quiz result records: retained until user deletion request or retention policy expiry.
- Payment and entitlement records: retained for accounting, fraud prevention, and dispute handling.
- Security logs: retained according to security policy and operational requirements.

## 8. User Rights
Where applicable (e.g., GDPR/CCPA), users may request:
- Access
- Correction
- Deletion
- Restriction/objection
- Portability (where technically feasible)

Submit requests via privacy contact channel.

## 9. Cookies and Consent
Cookies/trackers are used for core functionality and, where configured, analytics/ads.
Consent choices affect non-essential processing.
See `CONSENT_AND_DATA_USE.md` for implementation details.

## 10. Security
We use technical and organizational controls including authentication, authorization, and operational monitoring.
No system can guarantee absolute security.

## 11. Children
Service is not intended for children under the minimum age required by applicable law.

## 12. Changes to this Policy
We may update this policy. Material changes are published with updated effective date.
