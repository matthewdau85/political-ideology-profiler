# Ad Compliance Checklist

Date: 2026-03-07

## Policy and consent
- [ ] Privacy policy discloses ad cookies and consent model.
- [ ] Consent banner behavior documented by region.
- [ ] Non-personalized ad fallback documented.

## Content safety
- [ ] Sensitive political content review process documented.
- [ ] User-generated share content moderation standards documented.
- [ ] Misleading/deceptive ad placement patterns avoided.

## Operations
- [ ] Incident runbook for policy violations.
- [ ] Owner contact and escalation process.
- [ ] Monthly policy review cadence.

## Platform readiness
- AdSense baseline: conditionally ready pending checklist completion and counsel sign-off.
