# REPORT ENGINE

## Premium Report Structure
1. Cluster interpretation
2. Economic axis reading
3. Social axis reading
4. Historical figure nearest-neighbor discussion
5. Country party alignment snapshot
6. Internal ideological tension flags
7. Predicted policy preference narrative
8. Evolution scenarios over time

## Quality Rules
- Narrative must remain consistent with user scores.
- Include uncertainty language where differences are marginal.
- Avoid deterministic wording.

## Example Positioning Snippet
"Your profile combines economically interventionist preferences with moderate social pluralism, placing you closest to social-democratic reform traditions."
