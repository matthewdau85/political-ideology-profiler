# Research Limitations

## Intended Use
Educational and analytical exploration of political preference patterns.

## Not Intended For
- Clinical diagnosis
- Legal/adjudicative decisions
- Deterministic identity assignment

## Key Limitations
- Heuristic model, not a validated psychometric instrument.
- Sampling and self-selection bias may affect aggregate interpretation.
- Cultural/contextual differences reduce cross-country comparability.
- Derived typology fit is not equivalent to statistical confidence.

## Interpretation Guidance
Use outputs as directional signals and discussion prompts, not definitive classification.
