# PERSONA REVIEW SUMMARY

## Consensus strengths
- Security posture improved with server-side entitlement checks and webhook validation.
- Product has credible engagement loops (quiz -> results -> sharing -> premium).
- Documentation now supports engineering and editorial due diligence.

## Consensus weaknesses
- Methodology remains heuristic and needs external validation studies for academic citation.
- Redis-based aggregates are practical but should evolve to warehouse-grade pipelines for large-scale research products.
- Additional anti-bot controls (CAPTCHA/risk scoring) are still recommended.

## Disagreements
- Product/growth personas support launch in limited beta now.
- Academic and legal personas recommend stronger calibration and policy disclosures before broad media positioning.

## Launch verdict
**Good for limited public beta with monetization enabled**. Full-scale research-grade public launch should follow validation and governance hardening.
